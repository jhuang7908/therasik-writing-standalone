## 4. The Hallmark Paradox

Does increasing Global Humanization lead to loss of Camelid Hallmarks?
  - Global Human % vs Camelid Hallmark Count: rho = -0.155, p = 0.5253
  → NO Correlation: Even highly humanized VHHs retain camelid hallmarks.

## 5. FINAL CONCLUSIONS ON VHH HUMANIZATION DRIVERS

1. STRATEGY LABEL VALIDITY:
   - ✅ Strategy labels correlate with sequence differences.

2. STRUCTURAL DRIVERS:
   - ❌ CDR2 Canonical Fold does not significantly drive FR2 humanization.

3. HALLMARK CONSERVATION:
   - Camelid hallmarks are retained INDEPENDENTLY of humanization level.
   - They are structural necessities, not variable features.
