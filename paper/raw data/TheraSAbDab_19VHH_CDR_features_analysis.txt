## 3. CDR3 Length Detailed Analysis

CDR3 is highly variable (5-21 aa) and key to antigen specificity.

CDR3 length groups vs strategy:
  Short (≤10) (n=3): BM=2, SR=1, Native=0
  Medium (11-15) (n=5): BM=3, SR=1, Native=1
  Long (≥16) (n=11): BM=3, SR=5, Native=3

## 7. Key Findings

1. CDR1 is highly conserved in length (17/19 molecules = 8 aa)
2. CDR2 length correlates with H2 canonical fold:
   - H2-9-1: predominantly 7 aa
   - H2-10-1: predominantly 8 aa
3. CDR3 is highly variable (5-21 aa):
   - Shortest: Enristomig (5 aa)
   - Longest: Caplacizumab, Envafolimab, Erfonrilimab (21 aa)
4. No strong association between CDR3 length and humanization strategy
5. All 3 approved VHHs have CDR3 ≥ 8 aa (suggesting minimum CDR3 length for success)
