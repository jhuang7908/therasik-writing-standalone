## 6. Key Findings

1. FR sequences are HIGHLY SIMILAR across all 19 molecules (>90% identity)
2. FR sequences do NOT cluster by strategy (BM/SR/Native)
3. All molecules have SIMILAR low germline identity (~30% to both human and camelid)

CONCLUSION:
  - Strategy labels (BM/SR/Native) are NOT based on FR germline identity
  - Possible basis:
    a) Specific residue modifications at known VHH positions (37, 44, 45, 47)
    b) Surface-exposed residue humanization patterns
    c) T-cell epitope removal strategies
    d) Patent/literature claims about engineering approach
  - Table1 'human_identity' (85-97%) likely reflects:
    * Global sequence similarity to nearest human VH (not FR-specific)
    * CDR sequence optimization
    * Surface residue humanization
