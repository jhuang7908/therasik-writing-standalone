## 3. Humanization Strategy Recommendations
### Strategy A: The 'Universal' Coat (IGHV3-23)
- **Target:** VHHs with standard CDR lengths (e.g., 8-8-X) and high Vernier identity to IGHV3-23.
- **Method:** CDR Grafting onto IGHV3-23. Retain VHH residues at positions 37, 44, 45, 47 (The VHH Tetrad) if solubility drops.

### Strategy B: The 'Bulky' Coat (IGHV3-53 / IGHV3-66)
- **Target:** VHHs with longer CDR1/2 or specific hydrophobic cores.
- **Method:** Use IGHV3-53/66 which naturally accommodate hydrophobic CDRH3s better.
