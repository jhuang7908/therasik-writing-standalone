§3 Conclusion & Recommendation

The 4-way comparison confirms that Smart-CMC (DeepFR-CTX) is the optimal path for this VHH. It successfully repairs the donor's instability while minimizing the structural impact of the humanizing F37Y mutation.

  • DeepFR-CTX: Successfully protects critical Vernier and structural sites, leading to a stable, high-naturalness candidate.
  • Surface Reshaping: Too aggressive for this scaffold; leads to significant structural deviation (4.23 Å H3 RMSD) and fails to improve stability.
  • Final Recommendation: Proceed with the DeepFR-CTX Optimized candidate. The H3 deviation (2.78 Å) is a necessary trade-off for the F37Y hallmark mutation, but is managed significantly better than with alternative methods.
