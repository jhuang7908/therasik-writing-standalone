Design Rationale: V3 is a structure-guided redesign on the validated IGHV3-9*01/IGKV3-18*02 scaffold. The complex structure (Dog_NGF_Ab_V2, Boltz-2 ipTM 0.724) was used to identify which framework positions are in direct NGF contact or maintain CDR loop geometry. Only three back-mutations are structurally justified: H71K (3 direct NGF atom contacts), H78F (CDR-H2 aromatic packing), and H93Y (CDR-H3 N-terminal anchor). H48 was reverted from human I to dog-native L (conservative, no structural risk) to reduce immunogenic load. All 6 changes vs V2 are in FR regions remote from CDRs and the antigen interface.

Key patent claims basis:
(1) Novel germline combination: IGHV3-9*01 × IGKV3-18*02 has no precedent in approved dog anti-NGF antibodies.

(4) CMC-optimized sequence: V3 eliminates the DT isomerization motif (pos73 T→S) vs Dog_V2, creating a distinct and CMC-improved sequence entity.

§9  Advisory & Recommendations
[PASS] CDR fidelity: All 6 CDRs 100% preserved. Binding epitope on dog NGF is maintained.
[PASS] CMC — DT isomerization resolved: V3 eliminates the DT motif at pos73 present in V2 via T→S (safe, conservative).
[PASS] CMC — Met oxidation resolved: VL M4→L eliminates the only Met oxidation risk in the Fv region.
[PASS] Back-mutation count reduced: From 4 to 3 true FR back-mutations, reducing overall immunogenic footprint vs dog immune system.
[PASS] Dog FR4 applied: Both VH and VL now use dog-canonical FR4 sequences, maximizing caninization completeness.
[WARN] Fv pI = 6.18: Slightly below the 6.5 formulation target. No safe sequence-level correction identified. Recommend pH 5.5 acetate buffer formulation, HSA-free conditions, and confirm actual pI by IEF or capillary isoelectric focusing post-expression.
[WARN] Instability Index = 43.9: Marginally above 40.0 threshold. Primarily driven by aromatic-dense CDR-H3 (GGYWYATSYYFDY). This is an inherited donor property and correlates poorly with actual thermostability for aromatic-rich CDR antibodies. Recommend Tm measurement by DSF/nanoDSF.
[WARN] Residual CDR-H2 liabilities (NS, DG): Inherited from human Tanezumab CDR-H2. Cannot be modified without redesigning CDRs. Monitor deamidation by peptide mapping after 4-week 40°C accelerated stability. Precedent: parent Tanezumab showed acceptable stability in phase III trials.
[INFO] Structure prediction: Recommend submitting V3 VH/VL sequences to Boltz-2 or ABodyBuilder2 for Fv homology model verification before synthesis ordering.
[INFO] Expression recommendation: Express as dog IgG-D or IgG-B scaffold (matched to anti-pain indication; low effector function preferred). Signal peptide: MGWSCIILFLVATATGVHS (standard).
