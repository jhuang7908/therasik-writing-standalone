Interpretation guidance

  • WARN = virtual checks completed with cautions; item is deliverable but requires confirmatory experimental assays before lead nomination.
  • PASS = no blocking virtual cautions; experimental confirmation remains required.
  • Naturalness WARN reflects an unusual VH/VL pairing likelihood in silico — does not predict binding loss.
  • mini-CMC REVIEW flags sequence-level liability motifs; their impact requires wet-lab confirmation.

§13 — Glossary

  • HPR — Human Peptide Repertoire Index: compatibility of variable-region 9-mers with a human antibody repertoire reference.
  • mini-CMC — light developability descriptors: pI, GRAVY, instability, liability motifs. Not equivalent to full formulation CMC.
  • Paired Fv naturalness — paired AbLang2-based plausibility of VH/VL co-occurrence in human repertoire.
  • CDR RMSD — Cα backbone deviation between donor and candidate modeled structures.
  • Delivery ZIP — README + FASTA (donor & candidate) + HTML report + result.json + optional Fv PDBs.
