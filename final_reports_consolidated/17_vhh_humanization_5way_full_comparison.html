§3 Conclusion & Recommendation

The 5-way comparison demonstrates that DeepFR-CTX is the superior core algorithm. While open-source methods like Llamanade and Surface Reshaping are useful for general humanization, they lack the context-specific structural protection required for complex VHH engineering involving hallmark mutations like F37Y.

  • DeepFR-CTX: Best stability (26.1), best structural preservation (2.78 Å), and highest naturalness.
  • Llamanade: Good naturalness, but moderate structural risk (3.62 Å) and no stability improvement.
  • Surface Reshaping: High structural risk (4.23 Å) and poor stability.
  • Final Recommendation: Use DeepFR-CTX as the primary engine for high-value VHH humanization projects. It provides a unique balance of developability and structural integrity that simpler frequency or exposure-based methods cannot match.
