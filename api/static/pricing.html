Frequently Asked Questions

Do unused credits roll over?
Pay-as-you-go credits never expire. Lab, Pro, and Company credits reset at the start of each billing cycle and do not roll over. New accounts receive 30,000 trial credits (36,000 with .edu email) plus 10 complimentary AI assistant turns. Coupon codes grant additional credits as defined per coupon.
Can I upgrade or cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time by contacting contact@insynbio.com. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
Is my antibody sequence data kept confidential?
Yes. Sequences are processed in-memory and purged after job completion. We do not use client sequences to train models or share them with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details. Enterprise customers can deploy on private infrastructure.
Do you offer academic or startup discounts?
Academic institutions and early-stage biotech startups may qualify for verified discount programs. Discounts are reviewed manually to keep self-service pricing aligned with market value.
Is there a money-back guarantee for first-time users?
Yes. Your first paid analysis on InSynBio (any standard humanization, CMC, or design workflow) is covered by a 100% money-back guarantee. If the output does not meet expectations, contact contact@insynbio.com within 14 days and we will refund the credit cost in full. This applies once per account and excludes offline VAM campaigns and Enterprise contracts.
Are offline VAM or wet-lab services included?
No. Lab, Pro, and Company plans cover ordinary online self-service analysis only. Full VAM campaigns, manual expert analysis, wet-lab experiments, and CRO execution are quoted separately.
Can I get an API key for programmatic access?
API access is available on Company and Enterprise plans. Contact contact@insynbio.com to request your API key and integration documentation.
